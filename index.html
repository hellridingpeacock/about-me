I am a singer songwriter from a small town called Baripada situated in Odisha. I play the guitar, sing and write songs. I have been quite successful in my journey so far getting selected as the one of the Top 24 artists in Nexa Music and have worked with A R Rahman and Clinton Cerejo. I also was ranked at the first position in the world in a competetion held by SKIO music for alternative songs. Currently I am in two musical projects namely Psychic Magicians and XY. I have plans to release a music video for the latest single of Psychic Magicians named Happy Place probably during August. The last video I released was a video of Show me the way by XY ehich also got featured in VH1 India. I also had edited these videos,so that's another skill that I have under my belt. I also love doing 3d work and vfx and playing VR games.
Now after my successful musical journey i have started getting into the world of Web3. Being creative in anything is what i enjoy and i can't wait to excel in this field as well!
